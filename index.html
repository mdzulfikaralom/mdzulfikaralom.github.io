    I am Dr. Zulfikar Alom, currently serving as a Computer and Information Research Scientist at the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at The University of Toledo, Ohio, USA, under the supervision of Prof. Liang Cheng . Previously, I held a postdoctoral research position at the University of Manitoba, Canada, under the supervision of Professors Cuneyt Gurcan Akcora and Murat Kantarcioglu .

    I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Università degli Studi dell’Insubria , Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Elena Ferrari and Prof. Barbara Carminati . My research interests span a broad range of areas, including Data Science, Cybersecurity and Privacy, the Internet of Things (IoT), Big Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Graph Neural Networks, Social Network Analysis, Malware Detection, AI-driven Drug Design and Modeling, and Bioinformatics/Biological Data Processing.
